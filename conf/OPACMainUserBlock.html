Library Refurbishments & Pop-up Library

We are currently undergoing a refurbishment which has meant we've had to close our usual Library space. A temporary 'pop-up' Library can now be found near the Refectory in KI001.

Your Library account!

Click login to your account in the top-right corner of this page to open the login pop-up.
Students must select the top option, labelled College login. The username is your College ID number only, with your password being your usual College password.
To request a new College password, please email library@cityplym.ac.uk where we can provide further guidance
